About Me

I am a Junior Computer Science student at the University of Michigan - Dearborn passionate about web development with experience in iOS design and development. I enjoy mentoring girls who are interested in STEM and planning events for the Girls Who Code chapter on our campus that highlight Sisterhood and Bravery. When I am not in class on campus, I help students plan their classes in the Engineering Lab Building office. I am also a student at the Apple Developer Academy where I learned game development and designing/creating solutions. I hope to take this knowledge with me and inspire young girls to solve community issues with technology.

My Projects

NBA PLayer

NBA Player was a game designed to help our client accomplish his long term goal of buying NBA stocks. This game simulates buying NBA stocks by by completing tasks and earning points as players do in tyconn games. The game was designed using SwiftUI and Xcode.

Clash Academy

Clash Academy is a fighting game inspired by the mentors at the Apple Academy. It is still ongoing and by the end my team hopes to accomplish a finishing game where players can choose a mentor to be their character and fight the CPU character in the game with a virtual joystick and commands to Jump, punch, block and run. The game is being designed with SpriteKit and Xcode.

Behavioral Trivia

Behavioral Trivia is a game designed to help people understand body language and queues so that they are prepared interacting in the real world. The game is a fun and interactive way to help users learn about their surroundings and themselves. The trivia was designed using SwiftUI and Xcode.
